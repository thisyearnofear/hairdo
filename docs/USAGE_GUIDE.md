# HAIRDO - AI Hairstyle Generator

## How to Use

### 1. Choose Your Photo
You have two options to provide a photo:
- **Upload Photo**: Click "Upload Photo" to select an image from your device
- **Take Selfie**: Click "Take Selfie" to use your device's camera

### 2. Select Hairstyle Options
- **Hairstyle**: Choose from various styles (Fade, Bob, Ponytail, etc.)
- **Shade**: Select the intensity (Light, Regular, Dark)
- **Color**: Pick a hair color (Blonde, Brown, Black, Red, etc.)

### 3. Transform Your Hairstyle
- Click "Transform Hairstyle" to generate your new look
- You'll need to pay a small fee (1 LISK) on Lisk blockchain
- Connect your wallet and approve the contract to spend your LSK tokens
- Confirm both the approval and payment transactions when prompted

### 4. View Results
- Your transformed hairstyle will appear in the results section
- You can download or share your new look

## Privacy & Security

- All photos are processed locally in your browser
- No images are stored on our servers
- Blockchain payments are secure and transparent
- Your personal data is never collected or sold

## Technical Details

- Powered by HairCLIP AI model on Replicate
- Built with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and Web3
- Deployed on Vercel with Lisk blockchain integration
